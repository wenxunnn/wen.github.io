Final File / Verdict

Who is the Murderer?

The website will only accept one name. Accuse someone only if the evidence connects motive, opportunity, and the attempted cover-up.

  • The body-temperature clue gives the likely death time.
  • The access log contradicts one person's alibi at that time.
  • The raw catalyst record shows who benefited from hiding the correction.
  • The deleted messages connect the same person to a threatening alias.
  • The researcher suspect has access to catalyst material, but the evidence points away from her.

Case Closed

Adrian Cole

Adrian Cole killed Clara Bell to stop her from revealing that the clean-energy sponsor story was built on a manipulated catalyst archive. The case is not proven by one clue alone; it is proven by the chain.

The Roman numeral lock opened the medical file. The medical file gave a body temperature of 29.65 °C at 11:00 PM. The invitation card placed the death in July, and the physical cooling-rate guide gives July as 1.05 °C/hour. The temperature drop is 7.35 °C, which means approximately 7 hours before the exam: 4:00 PM.

At that time, Adrian claimed he was upstairs on a sponsor call, but the guest-badge log placed him at the archive side exit at 4:02 PM. The sponsor archive then showed that the NI-2289 public label was false and that Adrian's portal was attached to the corrected raw record Clara planned to expose. Finally, the recovered messages tied the threatening alias terrace_step to Adrian's own sponsor notes.

Rina is the researcher suspect and had access to catalyst samples, but her clue points to sample preparation, not the murder. Marcus had technical access, and Lila had event pressure, but only Adrian had motive, opportunity, and a cover-up trail.
