Final File / Verdict

Who is the Murderer?

The website will only accept one name. Accuse someone only if the evidence connects motive, opportunity, and the attempted cover-up.

  • The body-temperature clue gives the likely death time.
  • The access log contradicts one person's alibi at that time.
  • The raw DFT record shows who edited the result Dr. Voss planned to expose.
  • The deleted messages connect the same person to a threatening alias.

Case Closed

Daniel Whitlock

Daniel Whitlock killed Dr. Mira Voss to stop her from revealing that he had manipulated the catalyst-screening archive. The case is not proven by one clue alone; it is proven by the chain.

The Roman numeral lock opened the medical file. The medical file gave a body temperature of 29.65 °C at 11:00 PM. The calendar scrap placed the death in July, and the physical cooling-rate guide gives July as 1.05 °C/hour. The temperature drop is 7.35 °C, which means approximately 7 hours before the exam: 4:00 PM.

At that time, Daniel claimed he was already at the seminar, but the access log placed him at the archive side exit at 4:02 PM. The research archive then showed that Daniel edited the raw NI-2289 record, the exact correction Dr. Voss planned to reveal. Finally, the recovered messages tied the threatening alias terrace_step to Daniel's own surface-science notes.

Rina had access to physical samples, Marcus had cluster privileges, and Priya had an authorship conflict. Each had suspicious details, but only Daniel had motive, opportunity, and a cover-up trail.
